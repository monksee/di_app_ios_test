About

Launched in June 2015, Dublin Inquirer is an independent newspaper which aims to provide quality coverage of city affairs.

It is published each Wednesday online, and each month in print.

This application is for the subscribers of Dublin Inquirer to provide a space for our readers and journalists to engage, and together, to better understand what is going on in the city.

Privacy Policy

This document explains how Dublin Inquirer processes information about you which is personally identifiable when you use the Dublin Inquirer app.

How we collect and use the information you provide to us?

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  • your name, your email address, month of birth, other registration or profile information and reporting information about your use of any Dublin Inquirer App services;
    Note: passwords are encrypted and cannot be seen by any staff members.
  • any other information you provide to us when using any Dublin Inquirer App services;
  • if you contact us, we may keep a record of that correspondence including emails and call recordings. This data will not be forwarded to any third party.
  • details about the device you use to access and/or use any Dublin Inquirer App services, which may include your IP address or other unique device identifiers;
  • information regarding your behaviour within the app (e.g page visits) for the purpose of Google Analytics statistics and to continue to try and improve your experience within the app.
  • other data that you choose to provide to us;

We use the information you provide to us in the following ways:

  • your contact details may be used to respond to any communication or request from you;
  • to monitor the use of any Dublin Inquirer App services;

How do we protect the information you provide to us?

All information you provide to us is stored on our secure servers. You are responsible for keeping confidential, the password you have chosen which enables you to access the app. We advise you not to share this password with anyone.

Unfortunately, the transmission of information over the internet is not always completely secure. We have taken measures to protect your information (e.g registration information and other), however we cannot guarantee the security of data transmitted to our service; any transmission of data is at your own risk.

Once we have received any information from you, we will use strict security features to try to prevent unauthorised third party access.

We may amend this privacy policy at any given time in order to reflect changes to any of Dublin Inquirer services. We will give you reasonable notice of any changes to the privacy policy.
